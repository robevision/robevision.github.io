Life reflects art.



I find the bridge between the past and the present to help preserve memories, families, and your stories. My passion lies in the archival of small town history. The history that goes unnoticed or can wilt away the easiest. To do this, I seek to help you in your preservation needs, whether it is a box of photos that needs to be digitized or a family member who might need a face to face to jot down pieces they remember of the past. History can help the future and also build your legacy. Let me help with this journey.
